Connect 4! Go head-to-head with a friend in a battle to own the grid!



Classic board game invented in 1974.



How to Play!
Click on a space to drop your disc into the grid.

Go head-to-head with another player and race to stack 4 in a row!

The first player to stack 4 in a row horizontally, vertically or diagonally, wins!
